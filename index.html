Transparent AI Detection

AIDetecto is a lightweight AI-detection prototype designed to show how responsible, transparent tools can help teams and investors evaluate written content.

We aim to provide clear, instantaneous signals about text origin—helping product managers, researchers, and decision-makers assess risk, audit outputs, and make trustable choices without fuss or noise.

Core Capabilities

Lightweight Prototype

A focused, client-side demonstration of core AI detection flows — no backend, no clutter. Ideal for rapid investor demos and early user testing.

Precision Analysis

Paste text, click Check Text, and receive an immediate, clear assessment. Shows the intended UX and decision logic without infrastructure overhead.

Transparent Mechanics

Simple, auditable JavaScript interactions illustrate how detection triggers and results are presented — perfect for technical review and product refinement.

Design-led Clarity

Minimal UI components emphasize signal over noise — readable typography, calm spacing, and a single, decisive CTA to guide conversions.

Investor-ready Metrics

Built to integrate real analytics later — the prototype maps where engagement and accuracy metrics will be captured to demonstrate growth potential.

User-first Accessibility

Keyboard-friendly controls and clear labels ensure the prototype communicates inclusivity and quality from the first interaction.

Get Started

Try the detector in seconds. Visit the Testing page to paste text and run a quick, client-side check. No signup, no noise — just a clear, immediate result to help you evaluate content authenticity.
