Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the tester?

Our tester provides a rapid, heuristic assessment based on surface-level patterns. It is designed for quick triage, not definitive attribution. Use results as an indicator, not proof.

What types of text can I check?

Short-form and long-form prose, emails, articles, and social posts. The tool focuses on English-language content and performs best on coherent, complete passages.

Does this detect a specific AI model?

No. This prototype signals likely AI-style patterns rather than identifying a particular model or service.

Can I rely on the result for legal or academic decisions?

No. Results are advisory and not admissible for formal proceedings. For high-stakes decisions, pair this tool with human review and forensic analysis.

How is my text handled?

This prototype runs locally in your browser and does not send your input to a server. In production, we recommend clear data-handling policies and optional client-side checks to preserve privacy.

Why did the tool mark human-written text as AI-generated?

Automated assessments can misclassify creative, edited, or formulaic writing. Common causes include polished stylistic fingerprints, short samples, or domain-specific language.

Will future versions be more reliable?

Yes. Our roadmap includes refined models, larger evaluation sets, transparency reports, and integrations for rigorous human-in-the-loop workflows.

How should I interpret the result?

Treat the output as a succinct signal: investigate further when flagged, and contextualize findings with source, revision history, and author verification.
